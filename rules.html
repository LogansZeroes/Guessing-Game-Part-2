Welcome to the Guessing Game

The official game manual i.e. "Rule Book"

There is a mystery number between 1 and 100

Your goal is to guess this number within 5 tries

Every round, you submit your guess

You will be told how close your guess is

Rinse and repeat until your 5 tries are up
